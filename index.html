What is Youthink?
A journal for secondary school thinkers

Youthink publishes essays, analyses, poems and creative pieces by Grades 7–12 students across 7 disciplines.

No minimum length — depth and originality matter most. Chinese, English, or bilingual welcome. We reply within 2–3 weeks.

How to Submit
Four steps to publishing your essay
1
Click Submit in the nav bar above.
2
Choose your track: Professional or Creative.
3
Fill in your details, paste your essay. PDF links welcome.
4
Submit. We'll reply within 2–3 weeks with a decision and feedback.
Two Tracks Explained
Professional vs Creative
Professional Structured essays with clear thesis and evidence. Research, analysis, formal writing.
Creative Any format, any length. Poems, reflections, short analyses. Genuine insight is everything.
Both tracks reviewed equally. The only criterion: does this make us think differently?

Choose Your Track
Professional Track
Higher bar · Rigorous review
For structured, argument-driven essays with clear thesis and engagement with ideas.
Creative Track
Accessible bar · Depth rewarded
Personal essays, short analyses, poems, any format. Original thinking wins.
Professional Track — Review Criteria
Clear, defensible thesis with supporting arguments
Engagement with relevant ideas, texts, or evidence
Logical structure and coherent development
Original insight beyond summary
Creative Track — Review Criteria
Depth and originality of thinking
Genuine personal voice or perspective
Ability to surprise or move the reader
Any format welcome: prose, poem, hybrid
